On March 10, at San Francisco's Ferry Building, we put four Python open source maintainers on a stage and asked them to stop being polite about what AI is doing to their projects. Guido van Rossum, creator of Python. [Sebastián Ramirez](https://github.com/tiangolo), creator of [FastAPI](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/). [Jeremiah Lowin](https://github.com/jlowin), CEO of Prefect and creator of [FastMCP](https://github.com/prefecthq/fastmcp). [Samuel Colvin](https://github.com/samuelcolvin), creator of [Pydantic](https://github.com/pydantic/). The panel was moderated by me, and part of [PyAI](https://pyai.events/), Pydantic's first conference, co-organised with Prefect.
