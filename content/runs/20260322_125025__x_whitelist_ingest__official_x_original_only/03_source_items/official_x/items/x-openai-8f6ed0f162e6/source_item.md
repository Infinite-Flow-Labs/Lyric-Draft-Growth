Today we’re releasing GPT‑5.4 mini and nano, our most capable small models yet. They bring many of the strengths of GPT‑5.4 to faster, more efficient models designed for high-volume workloads. GPT‑5.4 nano is the smallest, cheapest version of GPT‑5.4 for tasks where speed and cost matter most. It is also a significant upgrade over GPT‑5 nano.
